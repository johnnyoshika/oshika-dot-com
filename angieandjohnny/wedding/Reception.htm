The Reception will be held at Hollyburn Country Club in West Vancouver, 950 Crosscreek Road.

To get there, we suggest you take the Lion's Gate Bridge to Marine Drive in West Vancouver. Turn right on 15th Street, which will go uphill and become Crosscreek Road.   You'll find Hollyburn on your right-hand-side; parking is available for guests at the very end of the lane.
