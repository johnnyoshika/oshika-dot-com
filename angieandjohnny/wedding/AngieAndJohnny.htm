We met in the summer of 2001.   A mutual friend introduced us at a dinner party, and we were curious about each other right away. Friendship bloomed by email, and we consider our relationship to have started a couple months later, on October 31st.

When we're married in April 2004, we'll have been together for 2 1/2 years.   It's been an incredible whirlwind of laughter and companionship, supporting each other through frustrations and challenges, and expanding each other's horizons.  We've travelled together throughout BC and to Alberta, Phoenix, the Grand Canyon, Las Vegas, LA,  cruising in Mexico, and Peru.  We can't wait for the next journey!

In the coming years, we plan to gain valuable experience from our current jobs and then attend grad school.   We're both excited to start a family, when the time is right, own a home, and adopt a puppy.
