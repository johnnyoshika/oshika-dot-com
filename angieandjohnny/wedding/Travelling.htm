As Vancouver is an extremely popular tourist destination, you probably won't have any trouble finding a flight to the city.  WestJet and Air Canada have many daily flights from all over Canada, and international carriers bring guests from around the world.

Car Rental

Unless you plan to stay for a longer visit or leave Vancouver's downtown core frequently, we suggest you don't rent a car while in Vancouver. Traffic is often slow and parking is usually around $16 - 22 per day.

Companies Located at the Airport

Car rental facilities are right outside the terminal on the ground floor of the parkade:
  • Budget Rent-a-Car
  • Avis Rent-a-Car
  • Alamo Rent-a-Car
  • National Car Rentals
  • Hertz Canada
  • Thrifty Car Rental

Companies Close to the Airport
Off-airport car rental companies serving the airport include:

  • Discount Car and Truck
  • Dollar Rent-a-Car
  • Enterprise Rent-a-Car
These companies provide a free pick-up service from the airport to the nearby rental location.
