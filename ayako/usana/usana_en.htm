Inferior manufacturing can destroy even the greatest product formulations. For this reason, not only does USANA Health Sciences manufacture its own nutritional supplements, the company also voluntarily meets pharmaceutical Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP). The standards for pharmaceutical GMP are far more stringent than the food GMP requirements set by the U.S. government for supplements.
To adhere to the exacting standards of pharmaceutical GMP, and to ensure the quality of every product manufactured, USANA tests the raw ingredients before they are mixed, the product during manufacturing, and for a final time before anything is sent to the customer. USANA's Quality Assurance team keeps track of every detail, from testing and evaluation of raw materials, to what happens to the products during shipping and storage.
