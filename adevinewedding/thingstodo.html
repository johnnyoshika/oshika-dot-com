Things to Do in Vancouver

When you're in Vancouver, you may enjoy the opportunity to explore the city. We're proud of the natural beauty and cultural diversity of our city, and invite you to check out some of our attractions.  If you need more information, we'd be glad to give you suggestions for  what to see and how to get around.  Also, if you're interested in doing something that isn't listed below, just send us an email and we'll be glad to provide you with some information.
