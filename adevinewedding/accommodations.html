Accomodations

Reserve your accomodations early!

Saturday, August the 5th is right in the middle of one of the busiest holiday weekends on the Sunshine coast, and so we strongly recommend that you book your accommodations as soon as possible.

Bed and Breakfasts

The following links provide lists of Bed & Breakfasts and Cottages in the towns closest to the wedding. The towns of Roberts Creek and Davis Bay are each within walking distance or a 5-10 minute drive to the wedding. The towns of Gibsons and Sechelt are 10-20 minutes driving distance.

Campgrounds

If you are looking for campgrounds, you might try this campsite, which accepts reservations:

Porpoise Bay Provincial Park

To reserve, call Discover Camping at 1-800-689-9025, quote Campground #37.

Click here for more information on fees and services.
